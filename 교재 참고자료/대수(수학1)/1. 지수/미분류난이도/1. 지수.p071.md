133) [정답] 7
[해설]
$$\begin{array}{l}
\left(x^{\frac{1}{3}}+x^{-\frac{2}{3}}\right)^{3}+\left(x^{\frac{1}{3}}-x^{-\frac{2}{3}}\right)^{3} \\
=\left\{\left(x^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)^{3}+3\left(x^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)^{2} x^{-\frac{2}{3}}+3 x^{\frac{1}{3}}\left(x^{-\frac{2}{3}}\right)^{2}+\left(x^{-\frac{2}{3}}\right)^{3}\right\} \\
\quad \quad+\left\{\left(x^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)^{3}-3\left(x^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)^{2} x^{-\frac{2}{3}}+3 x^{\frac{1}{3}}\left(x^{-\frac{2}{3}}\right)^{2}-\left(x^{-\frac{2}{3}}\right)^{3}\right\} \\
=\left(x+3+3 x^{-1}+x^{-2}\right)+\left(x-3+3 x^{-1}-x^{-2}\right) \\
=2\left(x+3 x^{-1}\right)=2\left(x+\frac{3}{x}\right)
\end{array}$$
$x=2$ 를 대입하면
$$2\left(2+\frac{3}{2}\right)=7$$
134) [정답] 54
[해설]
$5^{x}+5^{1-x}=8$ 의 양변을 제곱하면
$$\begin{array}{l}
5^{2 x}+2 \times 5^{x} \times 5^{1-x}+5^{2(1-x)}=64 \\
\left(5^{2}\right)^{x}+2 \times 5+\left(5^{2}\right)^{1-x}=64 \\
25^{x}+10+25^{1-x}=64 \\
\therefore 25^{x}+25^{1-x}=54
\end{array}$$
[다른 풀이]
$5^{x}=t(t>0)$ 으로 놓으면 $5^{1-x}=\frac{5}{t}$ 이므로
$$\begin{aligned}
25^{x}+25^{1-x} & =\left(5^{x}\right)^{2}+\left(5^{1-x}\right)^{2}=t^{2}+\frac{25}{t^{2}} \\
& =\left(t+\frac{5}{t}\right)^{2}-2 \times 5=8^{2}-10=54
\end{aligned}$$
135) [정답] 6

$\sqrt{x}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}=3$ 의 양변을 제곱하면
$$\begin{array}{l}
x+2+\frac{1}{x}=9, x+\frac{1}{x}=7 \\
\therefore x+x^{-1}=7
\end{array}$$
(ㄱ)의 양변을 제곱하면 $x^{2}+2+x^{-2}=49$
$$\begin{array}{l}
\therefore x^{2}+x^{-2}=47 \\
\therefore \frac{x^{2}+x^{-2}+7}{x+x^{-1}+2}=\frac{47+7}{7+2}=6
\end{array}$$

137) [정답] $\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$
[해설]
$x^{\frac{1}{2}}+x^{-\frac{1}{2}}=2 \sqrt{2}$ 의 양변을 제곱하면
$$\begin{array}{l}
x+2+x^{-1}=8 \\
\therefore x+x^{-1}=6
\end{array}$$
$x^{\frac{1}{2}}+x^{-\frac{1}{2}}=2 \sqrt{2}$ 의 양변을 세제곱하면
$$\begin{array}{l}
x^{\frac{3}{2}}+x^{-\frac{3}{2}}+3\left(x^{\frac{1}{2}}+x^{-\frac{1}{2}}\right)=16 \sqrt{2} \\
x^{\frac{3}{2}}+x^{-\frac{3}{2}}+6 \sqrt{2}=16 \sqrt{2} \\
\therefore x^{\frac{3}{2}}+x^{-\frac{3}{2}}=10 \sqrt{2} \\
\therefore \frac{x^{\frac{3}{2}}+x^{-\frac{3}{2}}}{x+x^{-1}+14}=\frac{10 \sqrt{2}}{6+14}=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}
\end{array}$$
