[해설]
$$\begin{array}{l} 
\sqrt{a^{2} \times \sqrt{a \times \sqrt[3]{a^{4}}}}=\sqrt{a^{2}} \times \sqrt{\sqrt{a}} \times \sqrt{\sqrt{\sqrt[3]{a^{4}}}} \\
=\sqrt{a^{2}} \times \sqrt[4]{a} \times \sqrt[12]{a^{4}} \\
=a \times a^{\frac{1}{4}} \times a^{\frac{1}{3}} \\
=a^{1+\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{3}}=a^{\frac{19}{12}} \\
\sqrt[3]{\frac{\sqrt[4]{a^{n}}}{\sqrt{a^{5}}}}=\frac{\sqrt[3]{\sqrt[4]{a^{n}}}}{\sqrt[3]{\sqrt{a^{5}}}}=\frac{\sqrt[12]{a^{n}}}{\sqrt[6]{a^{5}}}=\frac{a^{\frac{n}{12}}}{a^{\frac{5}{6}}} \\
=a^{\frac{n}{12}-\frac{5}{6}}=a^{\frac{n-10}{12}}
\end{array}$$

따라서 $a^{\frac{19}{12}}=a^{\frac{n-10}{12}}$ 이므로
$19=n-10 \quad \therefore n=29$
