58) [정답] $-\frac{75}{4}$
[해설]
$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{\alpha^{-1}-\beta^{-1}}{\alpha^{-2}-\beta^{-2}} & =\frac{\alpha^{-1}-\beta^{-1}}{\left(\alpha^{-1}+\beta^{-1}\right)\left(\alpha^{-1}-\beta^{-1}\right)} \\
& =\frac{1}{\alpha^{-1}+\beta^{-1}}=\frac{1}{\frac{1}{\alpha}+\frac{1}{\beta}} \\
& =\frac{\alpha \beta}{\alpha+\beta}
\end{aligned}$$

이때 이차방정식 $x^{2}+2 k x+6=0$ 의 두 근이 $\alpha, \beta$ 이므로 근과 계수의 관계에 의하여
$$\begin{array}{l}
\alpha+\beta=-2 k, \alpha \beta=6 \\
\therefore \frac{\alpha^{-1}-\beta^{-1}}{\alpha^{-2}-\beta^{-2}}=\frac{\alpha \beta}{\alpha+\beta}=\frac{6}{-2 k}=-\frac{3}{k}
\end{array}$$

따라서 $-\frac{3}{k}=\frac{4}{25}$ 이므로
$$k=-\frac{75}{4}$$
