140) [정답] $\frac{13}{4}$
[해설]
$\frac{3^{3 x}+3^{-3 x}}{3^{x}+3^{-x}}=\frac{3^{x}\left(3^{3 x}+3^{-3 x}\right)}{3^{x}\left(3^{x}+3^{-x}\right)}=\frac{\left(3^{2 x}\right)^{2}+3^{-2 x}}{3^{2 x}+1}=\frac{13}{4}$
141) [정답] $\frac{9}{11}$
[해설]
$\frac{a^{x}-a^{-x}}{a^{x}+a^{-x}}$ 의 분모, 분자에 각각 $a^{x}$ 을 곱하면
$\frac{a^{x}-a^{-x}}{a^{x}+a^{-x}}=\frac{a^{x}\left(a^{x}-a^{-x}\right)}{a^{x}\left(a^{x}+a^{-x}\right)}=\frac{a^{2 x}-1}{a^{2 x}+1}$
$$=\frac{10-1}{10+1}=\frac{9}{11}$$
[다른 풀이]
$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{a^{x}-a^{-x}}{a^{x}+a^{-x}} & =\frac{a^{-x}\left(a^{2 x}-1\right)}{a^{-x}\left(a^{2 x}+1\right)}=\frac{a^{2 x}-1}{a^{2 x}+1} \\
& =\frac{10-1}{10+1}=\frac{9}{11}
\end{aligned}$$

143) [정답] $5 \sqrt{5}$
