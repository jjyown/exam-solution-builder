127) [정답] $\frac{81}{16}$
[해설]
$2^{a}=x, 2^{b}=y, 2^{c}=z$ 라 하면 $a+b+c=-1$ 이므로
$$\begin{aligned}
x y z & =2^{a} 2^{b} 2^{c}=2^{a+b+c} \\
& =2^{-1}=\frac{1}{2}
\end{aligned}$$
$2^{a}+2^{b}+2^{c}=\frac{13}{4}$ 이므로
$$x+y+z=\frac{13}{4}$$
$2^{-a}+2^{-b}+2^{-c}=\frac{11}{2}$ 이므로
$\frac{1}{x}+\frac{1}{y}+\frac{1}{z}=\frac{11}{2}$
$$\begin{array}{l}
\frac{x y+y z+z x}{x y z}=\frac{11}{2} \\
2(x y+y z+z x)=\frac{11}{2}(\because \text { (ㄱ) }) \\
\begin{aligned}
\therefore x y+y z+z x & =\frac{11}{4} \\
\therefore 4^{a}+4^{b}+4^{c} & =\left(2^{2}\right)^{a}+\left(2^{2}\right)^{b}+\left(2^{2}\right)^{c} \\
& =\left(2^{a}\right)^{2}+\left(2^{b}\right)^{2}+\left(2^{c}\right)^{2} \\
& =x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2} \\
& =(x+y+z)^{2}-2(x y+y z+z x) \\
& =\left(\frac{13}{4}\right)^{2}-2 \times \frac{11}{4} \\
& =\frac{81}{16}
\end{aligned}
\end{array}$$
