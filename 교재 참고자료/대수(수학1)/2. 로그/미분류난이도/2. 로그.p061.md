55) [정답] 4
[해설]

$$\therefore 3^{\frac{x}{3}}=\left(3^{x}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}}=64^{\frac{1}{3}}=\left(4^{3}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}}=4$$
[다른 풀이]

$$\begin{array}{l}
\frac{x}{3}=\frac{1}{3} \log _{3} 64=\frac{1}{3} \log _{3} 4^{3}=\log _{3} 4 \\
\therefore 3^{\frac{x}{3}}=3^{\log _{3} 4}=4
\end{array}$$
