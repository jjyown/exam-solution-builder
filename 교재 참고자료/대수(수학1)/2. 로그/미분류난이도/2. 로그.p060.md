49) [정답] $\frac{3(2 a+1)}{4 a(a+1)}$
[해설]
$$\begin{aligned}
\sqrt{6 \sqrt{6}} & =\sqrt{6} \times \sqrt{\sqrt{6}}=\sqrt{6} \times \sqrt[4]{6} \\
& =6^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 6^{\frac{1}{4}}=6^{\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{4}}=6^{\frac{3}{4}}
\end{aligned}$$
$\sqrt{3 \sqrt{3}}=\sqrt{3} \times \sqrt{\sqrt{3}}=\sqrt{3} \times \sqrt[4]{3}=3^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 3^{\frac{1}{4}}=3^{\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{4}}=3^{\frac{3}{4}}$ 이므로
$$\begin{aligned}
\log _{3} \sqrt{6 \sqrt{6}}-\log _{6} \sqrt{3 \sqrt{3}} & =\log _{3} 6^{\frac{3}{4}}-\log _{6} 3^{\frac{3}{4}} \\
& =\frac{3}{4}\left(\log _{3} 6-\log _{6} 3\right) \\
& =\frac{3}{4}\left(\frac{\log _{2} 6}{\log _{2} 3}-\frac{\log _{2} 3}{\log _{2} 6}\right) \\
& =\frac{3}{4}\left\{\frac{\log _{2}(2 \times 3)}{\log _{2} 3}-\frac{\log _{2} 3}{\log _{2}(2 \times 3)}\right\} \\
& =\frac{3}{4}\left(\frac{1+\log _{2} 3}{\log _{2} 3}-\frac{\log _{2} 3}{1+\log _{2} 3}\right) \\
& =\frac{3}{4}\left(\frac{1+a}{a}-\frac{a}{1+a}\right) \\
& =\frac{3}{4} \times \frac{(1+a)^{2}-a^{2}}{a(a+1)} \\
& =\frac{3(2 a+1)}{4 a(a+1)}
\end{aligned}$$
