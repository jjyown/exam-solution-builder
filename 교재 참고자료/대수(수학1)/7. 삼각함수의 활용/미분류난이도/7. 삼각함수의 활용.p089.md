$\overline{\mathrm{OB}}^{2}+\overline{\mathrm{OC}}^{2}=\overline{\mathrm{BC}}^{2} \quad \therefore \quad \angle \mathrm{BOC}=\frac{\pi}{2}$
$\angle \mathrm{AOB}=\theta$ 라 하면
$S_{1}=\frac{1}{2} \cdot \sqrt{5} \cdot \sqrt{5} \cdot \sin \theta=\frac{5}{2} \sin \theta$
$\angle \mathrm{AOC}=2 \pi-\left(\theta+\frac{\pi}{2}\right)=\frac{3}{2} \pi-\theta$ 이므로
$S_{2}=\frac{1}{2} \cdot \sqrt{5} \cdot \sqrt{5} \cdot \sin \left(\frac{3}{2} \pi-\theta\right)=-\frac{5}{2} \cos \theta$
이 때 $4 S_{1}=3 S_{2}$ 이므로
$4 \cdot \frac{5}{2} \sin \theta=3 \cdot\left(-\frac{5}{2} \cos \theta\right)$
$\therefore \sin \theta=-\frac{3}{4} \cos \theta$
이 때 $\sin ^{2} \theta+\cos ^{2} \theta=1$ 이므로
$\left(-\frac{3}{4} \cos \theta\right)^{2}+\cos ^{2} \theta=1$
$\frac{25}{16} \cos ^{2} \theta=1 \quad \therefore \quad \cos ^{2} \theta=\frac{16}{25}$
$\frac{\pi}{2}<\theta<\pi$ 이므로 $\cos \theta=-\frac{4}{5}$

$$\begin{array}{l}
\overline{\mathrm{AB}}^{2}=(\sqrt{5})^{2}+(\sqrt{5})^{2}-2 \cdot \sqrt{5} \cdot \sqrt{5} \cdot \cos \theta \\
\quad=5+5-2 \cdot \sqrt{5} \cdot \sqrt{5} \cdot\left(-\frac{4}{5}\right)=18 \\

\end{array}$$
