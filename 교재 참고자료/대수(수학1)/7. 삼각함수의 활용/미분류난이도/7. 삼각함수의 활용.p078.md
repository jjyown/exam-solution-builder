74) [정답] (2)
[해설]
$\overline{\mathrm{BD}}: \overline{\mathrm{CD}}=\overline{\mathrm{AB}}: \overline{\mathrm{AC}}=2: 3$ 이므로 $\overline{\mathrm{BD}}=2 x, \overline{\mathrm{CD}}=3 x(x>0)$ 라 하고 $\angle \mathrm{BAD}=\theta$ 라 하면
$\triangle \mathrm{ABD}$ 에서 코사인법칙에 의하여
$$\cos \theta=\frac{10^{2}+(4 \sqrt{6})^{2}-(2 x)^{2}}{2 \cdot 10 \cdot 4 \sqrt{6}}=\frac{196-4 x^{2}}{80 \sqrt{6}}$$
$\triangle \mathrm{ADC}$ 에서 코사인법칙에 의하여
$$\cos \theta=\frac{(4 \sqrt{6})^{2}+15^{2}-(3 x)^{2}}{2 \cdot 4 \sqrt{6} \cdot 15}=\frac{321-9 x^{2}}{120 \sqrt{6}}$$

따라서 $\frac{196-4 x^{2}}{80 \sqrt{6}}=\frac{321-9 x^{2}}{120 \sqrt{6}}$ 이므로
$$\begin{array}{l}
98-2 x^{2}=107-3 x^{2}, x^{2}=9 \quad \therefore x=3(\because x>0) \\
\therefore \overline{\mathrm{BD}}=2 x=6
\end{array}$$
75) [정답] 5
[해설]
$\triangle \mathrm{ABC}$ 에서
$$\cos B=\frac{\overline{\mathrm{AB}}}{\overline{\mathrm{BC}}}=\frac{c}{3 n}, \cos C=\frac{\overline{\mathrm{AC}}}{\overline{\mathrm{BC}}}=\frac{b}{3 n}$$
$\triangle \mathrm{ABP}$ 에서 코사인법칙에 의하여
$$\begin{aligned}
l^{2} & =n^{2}+c^{2}-2 \cdot n \cdot c \cdot \cos B \\
& =n^{2}+c^{2}-2 n c \cdot \frac{c}{3 n} \\
& =n^{2}+\frac{1}{3} c^{2}
\end{aligned}$$
$\triangle \mathrm{AQC}$ 에서 코사인법칙에 의하여
$$\begin{aligned}
m^{2} & =n^{2}+b^{2}-2 \cdot n \cdot b \cdot \cos C \\
& =n^{2}+b^{2}-2 n b \cdot \frac{b}{3 n} \\
& =n^{2}+\frac{1}{3} b^{2}
\end{aligned}$$
$\triangle \mathrm{ABC}$ 가 직각삼각형이므로
$$\begin{array}{l}
b^{2}+c^{2}=(3 n)^{2} \\
\begin{aligned}
\therefore l^{2}+m^{2} & =\left(n^{2}+\frac{1}{3} c^{2}\right)+\left(n^{2}+\frac{1}{3} b^{2}\right) \\
& =2 n^{2}+\frac{1}{3}\left(b^{2}+c^{2}\right) \\
& =2 n^{2}+\frac{1}{3} \cdot(3 n)^{2}=5 n^{2}
\end{aligned} \\
\therefore k=5
\end{array}$$
76) [정답] $\frac{16 \sqrt{3}}{3}$
[해설]

다음 그림과 같이 사각형 ABCD 가 원에 내접하므로
$$\begin{aligned}
\angle \mathrm{ABC} & =180^{\circ}-\angle \mathrm{ADC} \\
& =120^{\circ}
\end{aligned}$$

선분 AC 를 그으면
$$\triangle \mathrm{ABC}=\frac{1}{2} \times 3 \times 3 \times \sin 120^{\circ}=\frac{9 \sqrt{3}}{4}$$

삼각형 ABC 에서
$$\overline{\mathrm{AC}}^{2}=3^{2}+3^{2}-2 \times 3 \times 3 \times \cos 120^{\circ}=27 \text { 이므로 }$$

삼각형 ACD 에서
$$\begin{aligned}
\overline{\mathrm{AC}}^{2} & =\overline{\mathrm{CD}}^{2}+\overline{\mathrm{DA}}^{2}-2 \times \overline{\mathrm{CD}} \times \overline{\mathrm{DA}} \times \cos 60^{\circ} \\
& =\overline{\mathrm{CD}}^{2}+\overline{\mathrm{DA}}^{2}-\overline{\mathrm{CD}} \times \overline{\mathrm{DA}} \\
& =(\overline{\mathrm{CD}}+\overline{\mathrm{DA}})^{2}-3 \overline{\mathrm{CD}} \times \overline{\mathrm{DA}}
\end{aligned}$$
$27=64-3 \overline{\mathrm{CD}} \times \overline{\mathrm{DA}}, \overline{\mathrm{CD}} \times \overline{\mathrm{DA}}=\frac{37}{3}$
$\triangle \mathrm{ACD}=\frac{1}{2} \times \overline{\mathrm{CD}} \times \overline{\mathrm{DA}} \times \sin 60^{\circ}=\frac{37 \sqrt{3}}{12}$
따라서 $\square \mathrm{ABCD}=\triangle \mathrm{ABC}+\triangle \mathrm{ACD}=\frac{16 \sqrt{3}}{3}$

[해설]
$\square \mathrm{ABCD}$ 가 원에 내접하므로 $B+D=180^{\circ}$
$$\therefore \cos D=\cos \left(180^{\circ}-B\right)=-\cos B=-\frac{1}{9}$$

따라서 $\triangle \mathrm{DAC}$ 에서 코사인법칙에 의하여
$$\begin{array}{l} 
\overline{\mathrm{AC}}^{2}=3^{2}+6^{2}-2 \cdot 3 \cdot 6 \cdot \cos D \\
=9+36-2 \cdot 3 \cdot 6 \cdot\left(-\frac{1}{9}\right)=49 \\
\therefore \overline{\mathrm{AC}}=7
\end{array}$$
