\triangle \mathrm{PAU}= & \frac{1}{2} \overline{\mathrm{AU}} \overline{\mathrm{AP}} \sin \left(180^{\circ}-\theta\right) \\

141) [정답] (3)
[해설]
$\overline{\mathrm{BC}}=3 \overline{\mathrm{AD}}$ 에서 $\overline{\mathrm{BC}}: \overline{\mathrm{AD}}=3: 1$ 이므로
$\triangle \mathrm{ABC}: \triangle \mathrm{ACD}=3: 1$
즉 $\triangle \mathrm{ABC}=3 \triangle \mathrm{ACD}$ 이므로
$\frac{1}{2} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{AB}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{AC}} \cdot \sin \alpha=3 \cdot \frac{1}{2} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{AC}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{CD}} \cdot \sin \beta$
$$\therefore \frac{\sin \alpha}{\sin \beta}=\frac{3 \overline{\mathrm{CD}}}{\overline{\mathrm{AB}}}$$
