158) [정답] $2 \sqrt{3}$
[해설]
$$\begin{aligned}
\triangle \mathrm{ABC} & =\frac{1}{2} \times 4 \times 6 \times \sin 60^{\circ} \\
& =\frac{1}{2} \times 4 \times 6 \times \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}=6 \sqrt{3}
\end{aligned}$$

이때, 선분 PQ 에 의하여 삼각형 ABC 의 넓이가
이등분되므로
$$\begin{aligned}
\triangle \mathrm{APQ} & =\frac{1}{2} \times \overline{\mathrm{AP}} \times \overline{\mathrm{AQ}} \times \sin 60^{\circ} \\
& =\frac{1}{2} \times \overline{\mathrm{AP}} \times \overline{\mathrm{AQ}} \times \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}=\frac{1}{2} \times 6 \sqrt{3} \\
\therefore \overline{\mathrm{AP}} & \times \overline{\mathrm{AQ}}=12
\end{aligned}$$

또한, $\triangle \mathrm{APQ}$ 에서 코사인법칙에 의하여
$$\begin{aligned}
\overline{\mathrm{PQ}}^{2} & =\overline{\mathrm{AP}}^{2}+\overline{\mathrm{AQ}}^{2}-2 \times \overline{\mathrm{AP}} \times \overline{\mathrm{AQ}} \times \cos 60^{\circ} \\
& =\overline{\mathrm{AP}}^{2}+\overline{\mathrm{AQ}}^{2}-2 \times 12 \times \frac{1}{2} \\
& \geq 2 \sqrt{\overline{\mathrm{AP}}^{2} \times \overline{\mathrm{AQ}}^{2}}-12 \\
& =2 \overline{\mathrm{AP}} \times \overline{\mathrm{AQ}}-12 \\
& =2 \times 12-12=12
\end{aligned}$$

따라서 구하는 선분 PQ 의 길이의 최솟값은
$$\sqrt{12}=2 \sqrt{3}$$
