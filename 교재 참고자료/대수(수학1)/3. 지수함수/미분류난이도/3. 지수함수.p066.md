4) [정답] (2)
[해설]
ㄱ. $f(-m)=a^{-m}=\frac{1}{a^{m}}=\frac{1}{f(m)}$
ㄴ. $f(3 m)=a^{3 m}=\left(a^{m}\right)^{3}=\{f(m)\}^{3}$
ㄷ. $f(m n)=a^{m n}, f(m)+f(n)=a^{m}+a^{n}$ 이므로
$$f(m n) \neq f(m)+f(n)$$

이상에서 옳은 것은 ㄱ, ㄴ이다.
5) [정답] 10
[해설]
$$\begin{array}{l}
f(0)=a^{n}=5, f(2)=a^{2 m+n}=20 \text { 이므로 } \\
5 a^{2 m}=20, a^{2 m}=4 \\
\therefore a^{m}=2\left(\because a^{m}>0\right) \\
\therefore f(1)=a^{m+n}=a^{m} \cdot a^{n}=2 \cdot 5=10
\end{array}$$
6) [정답] $\frac{q}{p}$
[해설]
$f(a+b)=\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{a+b}=\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{a}\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{b}$
이 때 $p=f(-a)=\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{-a}=\left\{\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{a}\right\}^{-1}$ 이므로
$\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{a}=\frac{1}{p}$
또 $q=f(b)=\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{b}$ 이므로
$f(a+b)=\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{a}\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{b}=\frac{q}{p}$
7) [정답] (2)
[해설]
$y=a^{x}$ 의 그래프가 두 점 $(b, 2),(c, 6)$ 을 지나므로
$2=a^{b}, 6=a^{c}$
$$\begin{aligned}
\therefore f\left(\frac{b-c}{2}\right) & =a^{\frac{b-c}{2}}=\left(a^{b-c}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}=\left(\frac{a^{b}}{a^{c}}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}} \\
& =\left(\frac{2}{6}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{3}}=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{3}
\end{aligned}$$
8) [정답] (4)
[해설]
$f(p)=q$ 에서 $2^{p}=q$
$$\begin{aligned}
\therefore f\left(\frac{p}{2}\right)+f\left(-\frac{p}{2}\right) & =2^{\frac{p}{2}}+2^{-\frac{p}{2}} \\
& =\left(2^{p}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}+\left(2^{p}\right)^{-\frac{1}{2}} \\
& =\sqrt{q}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{q}}
\end{aligned}$$
