61) [정답] $3 \sqrt{2}$
[해설]
$\mathrm{A}\left(a, 3^{a}\right), \mathrm{B}\left(b, 3^{b}\right)$ 이고, 직선 AB 의 기울기가 3 이므로
$$\begin{array}{l}
\frac{3^{b}-3^{a}}{b-a}=3 \\
\therefore b-a=\frac{1}{3}\left(3^{b}-3^{a}\right)
\end{array}$$

또 $\overline{\mathrm{AB}}=2 \sqrt{5}$ 이므로 $(b-a)^{2}+\left(3^{b}-3^{a}\right)^{2}=(2 \sqrt{5})^{2}$
위의 식에 (ㄱ)을 대입하면
$$\begin{array}{l}
\frac{1}{9}\left(3^{b}-3^{a}\right)^{2}+\left(3^{b}-3^{a}\right)^{2}=20 \\
\left(3^{b}-3^{a}\right)^{2}=18 \\
\therefore 3^{b}-3^{a}=3 \sqrt{2}\left(\because 3^{a}<3^{b}\right)
\end{array}$$

64) [정답] $C<B<A$
[해설]
$A=a^{\frac{n+1}{n}}, B=a^{\frac{n+2}{n+1}}, C=a^{\frac{n+3}{n+2}}$
$\frac{n+1}{n}=1+\frac{1}{n}, \frac{n+2}{n+1}=1+\frac{1}{n+1}, \frac{n+3}{n+2}=1+\frac{1}{n+2}$ 이고
$n$ 이 자연수이므로
$\frac{1}{n+2}<\frac{1}{n+1}<\frac{1}{n}$
따라서 $1+\frac{1}{n+2}<1+\frac{1}{n+1}<1+\frac{1}{n}$, 즉
$\frac{n+3}{n+2}<\frac{n+2}{n+1}<\frac{n+1}{n}$ 이고 $a>1$ 이므로
$a^{\frac{n+3}{n+2}}<a^{\frac{n+2}{n+1}}<a^{\frac{n+1}{n}}$, 즉 $C<B<A$
