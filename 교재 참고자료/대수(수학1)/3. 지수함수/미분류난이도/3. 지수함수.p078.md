$f(x)=(x-4)^{2}-1$

$f(x)=(x-2)^{2}+b-4$

$b-4 \leq f(x) \leq b-3$

$\therefore a^{b-4}=81$

$\therefore a^{b-3}=9$

$a=\frac{1}{9}$ 을 (ㄴ)에 대입하면 $\left(\frac{1}{9}\right)^{b-3}=9$
$9^{-b+3}=9,-b+3=1 \quad \therefore b=2$
$\therefore a b=\frac{2}{9}$
