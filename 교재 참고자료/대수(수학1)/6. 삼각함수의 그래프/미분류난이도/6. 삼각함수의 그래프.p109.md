$\frac{\sin \theta_{1}}{\sin \theta_{2}}=\frac{4}{3}$ 에서
$\sin \theta_{1}=\frac{4}{3} \sin \theta_{2} \leq \frac{2 \sqrt{2}}{3}$
이므로 $\sin \theta_{2} \leq \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$
$\therefore 0<\theta_{2} \leq \frac{\pi}{4}\left(\because 0<\theta_{2}<\frac{\pi}{2}\right)$
