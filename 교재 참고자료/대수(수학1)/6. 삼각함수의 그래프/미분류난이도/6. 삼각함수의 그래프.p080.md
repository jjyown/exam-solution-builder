$$\begin{aligned}
y & =2 \cos (\pi x+\pi)-1 \\
& =2 \sin \left(\pi x+\pi+\frac{\pi}{2}\right)-1 \\
& =2 \sin \left(\pi x+\frac{3}{2} \pi\right)-1 \\
& =-2 \sin \left(-\pi x-\frac{3}{2} \pi\right)-1 \\
& =-2 \sin \left(-\pi x+\frac{\pi}{2}\right)-1
\end{aligned}$$

따라서 $p=\frac{1}{2}, q=-1$ 이다.
