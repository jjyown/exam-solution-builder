즉 $4 \theta=\pi$ 이므로
$$\begin{array}{l}
\cos 5 \theta=\cos (\pi+\theta)=-\cos \theta \\
\begin{array}{r}
\cos 6 \theta=\cos (\pi+2 \theta)=-\cos 2 \theta \\
\cos 7 \theta=\cos (\pi+3 \theta)=-\cos 3 \theta
\end{array} \\
\begin{array}{r}
\cos 8 \theta=\cos (\pi+4 \theta)=-\cos 4 \theta \\
\therefore \cos \theta+\cos 2 \theta+\cos 3 \theta+\cdots+\cos 9 \theta \\
=(\cos \theta+\cos 5 \theta)+(\cos 2 \theta+\cos 6 \theta)+(\cos 3 \theta+\cos 7 \theta) \\
\quad+(\cos 4 \theta+\cos 8 \theta)+\cos 9 \theta
\end{array} \\
\begin{array}{r}
=(\cos \theta-\cos \theta)+(\cos 2 \theta-\cos 2 \theta)+(\cos 3 \theta-\cos 3 \theta) \\

\end{array} \\
