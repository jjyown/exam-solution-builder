$${ }_{k+1} \mathrm{P}_{k}=(k+1)!$$

그러므로 $f(2 k+1)=k!\times(k+1)!$
(i), (ii)에서
$$\begin{array}{l}
f(2 k)=(k-1)!\times k!, f(2 k+1)=k!\times(k+1)! \\
45(f(n)+f(n+1))=f(n+2)
\end{array}$$
(ㄱ)에서 $n=2 k$ 인 경우
$$45(f(2 k)+f(2 k+1))=f(2 k+2)$$

$$f(2 k)>0, f(2 k+1)=f(2 k+2)=k!\times(k+1)!$$
