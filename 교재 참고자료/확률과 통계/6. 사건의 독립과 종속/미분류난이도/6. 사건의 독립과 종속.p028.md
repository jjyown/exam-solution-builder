$$\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{P}(\mathrm{X} \cap \mathrm{Y}) & =1-\mathrm{P}\left((X \cap Y)^{c}\right) \\
& =1-\mathrm{P}\left(X^{c} \cup Y^{c}\right) \\
& =1-\left\{\mathrm{P}\left(X^{c}\right)+\mathrm{P}\left(Y^{c}\right)-\mathrm{P}\left(X^{c} \cap Y^{c}\right)\right\}
\end{aligned}$$

독립시행의 확률에 의하여 $\mathrm{P}\left(X^{c}\right)={ }_{5} \mathrm{C}_{0}\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{0}\left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{5}=\frac{32}{243}$ 마찬가지로 $\mathrm{P}\left(\mathrm{Y}^{c}\right)=\frac{32}{243}$ 이다.
