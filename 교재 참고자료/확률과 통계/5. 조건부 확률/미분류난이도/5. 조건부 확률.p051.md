$$\mathrm{P}(A)=0.6, \mathrm{P}(B \mid A)=0.9, \mathrm{P}\left(B \mid A^{C}\right)=0.3$$

$$\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{P}(B) & =\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)+\mathrm{P}\left(A^{C} \cap B\right) \\

\end{aligned}$$
