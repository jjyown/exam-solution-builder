1) [정답] (5)
[해설]
$$\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{P}(A \cap B) & =\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)-\mathrm{P}(A \cup B) \\
& =\frac{2}{5}+\frac{4}{5}-\frac{9}{10} \\
& =\frac{3}{10}
\end{aligned}$$
$$\therefore \mathrm{P}(B \mid A)=\frac{\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)}{\mathrm{P}(A)}=\frac{\frac{3}{10}}{\frac{2}{5}}=\frac{3}{4}$$
2) [정답] (3)
[해설]
$\mathrm{P}(A \mid B)=\mathrm{P}(B \mid A)$ 에서 $\frac{\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)}{\mathrm{P}(B)}=\frac{\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)}{\mathrm{P}(A)}$
따라서 $\mathrm{P}(A)=\mathrm{P}(B)$
$\mathrm{P}(A \cup B)=\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)-\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)$ 에서
$1=2 \mathrm{P}(A)-\frac{1}{4}$
따라서 $\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{5}{8}$
3) [정답] (3)
[해설]
$$\mathrm{P}(A \mid B)=\frac{\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)}{\mathrm{P}(B)}=\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{1}{2}$$

에서 $\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)=\frac{1}{5}$ 이므로 $\quad \mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{\frac{1}{5}}{\frac{1}{2}}=\frac{2}{5}$
$$\begin{aligned}
\therefore \mathrm{P}(A \cup B) & =\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)-\mathrm{P}(A \cap B) \\
& =\frac{1}{2}+\frac{2}{5}-\frac{1}{5}=\frac{7}{10}
\end{aligned}$$
4) [정답]
(2)
[해설]
$\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{1}{3}, \mathrm{P}(B)=\frac{1}{6}$ 이므로
$\mathrm{P}(A \mid B)=\frac{\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)}{\mathrm{P}(B)}=\frac{\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)}{\frac{1}{6}}=6 \times \mathrm{P}(A \cap B)$
$\mathrm{P}(B \mid A)=\frac{\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)}{\mathrm{P}(A)}=\frac{\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)}{\frac{1}{3}}=3 \times \mathrm{P}(A \cap B)$
$\mathrm{P}(A \mid B)=\mathrm{P}(B \mid A)+\frac{1}{4}$ 에서
$6 \times \mathrm{P}(A \cap B)=3 \times \mathrm{P}(A \cap B)+\frac{1}{4}$
즉, $3 \times \mathrm{P}(A \cap B)=\frac{1}{4}$ 이므로
$\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)=\frac{1}{12}$
5) [정답]
(4)
[해설]
$\mathrm{P}(B)=1-\mathrm{P}\left(B^{C}\right)=1-\frac{3}{10}=\frac{7}{10}$ 이므로
$\mathrm{P}(A \cup B)=\mathrm{P}(A)+\mathrm{P}(B)-\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)$
$=\frac{2}{5}+\frac{7}{10}-\frac{1}{5}=\frac{9}{10}$
한편, $\mathrm{P}\left(A^{C} \cap B^{C}\right)=\mathrm{P}\left((A \cup B)^{C}\right)$
따라서
$\mathrm{P}\left(A^{C} \mid B^{C}\right)=\frac{\mathrm{P}\left(A^{C} \cap B^{C}\right)}{\mathrm{P}\left(B^{C}\right)}$
$$=\frac{\frac{1}{10}}{\frac{3}{10}}=\frac{1}{3}$$
6) [정답] $\frac{2}{3}$
[해설]
$$\mathrm{P}\left(A^{C} \mid B^{C}\right)=\frac{\mathrm{P}\left(A^{C} \cap B^{C}\right)}{\mathrm{P}\left(B^{C}\right)}=\frac{1-\mathrm{P}(A \cup B)}{1-\mathrm{P}(B)}=\frac{2}{3}$$
7) [정답]
[해설]
학생 200 명 중에서 임의로 선택한 1 명이 생태연구를 선택한 학생인 사건을 $A$, 여학생인 사건을 $B$ 라 하면 구하는 확률은 $\mathrm{P}(B \mid A)$ 이다.
$$\begin{array}{l}
\mathrm{P}(A)=\frac{110}{200}=\frac{11}{20}, \\
\mathrm{P}(A \cap B)=\frac{50}{200}=\frac{1}{4}
\end{array}$$
