62) [정답] 155
[해설]
확률변수 $X$ 가 정규분포 $\mathrm{N}\left(\mathrm{m}, \sigma^{2}\right)$ 을 따르므로 확률변수 $Z=\frac{X-m}{\sigma}$ 은 표준정규분포 $\mathrm{N}(0,1)$ 을 따른다.
$$\mathrm{P}(\mathrm{X} \leq 3)=\mathrm{P}\left(\frac{\mathrm{X}-\mathrm{m}}{\sigma} \leq \frac{3-\mathrm{m}}{\sigma}\right)=\mathrm{P}\left(\mathrm{Z} \leq \frac{3-\mathrm{m}}{\sigma}\right)=0.3$$

즉, $0.5-\mathrm{P}\left(0 \leq \mathrm{Z} \leq \frac{\mathrm{m}-3}{\sigma}\right)=0.3$ 이므로
$$\mathrm{P}\left(0 \leq \mathrm{Z} \leq \frac{\mathrm{m}-3}{\sigma}\right)=0.2 \text { 에서 } \frac{m-3}{\sigma}=0.52$$

즉, $m=3+0.52 \sigma \cdots \cdots$ (ㄱ)
이다. 이때
$$\begin{aligned}
P(3 \leq X \leq 80) & =P\left(\frac{3-m}{\sigma} \leq \frac{X-m}{\sigma} \leq \frac{80-m}{\sigma}\right) \\
& =P\left(\frac{3-m}{\sigma} \leq Z \leq \frac{80-m}{\sigma}\right) \\
& =P\left(\frac{3-m}{\sigma} \leq Z \leq 0\right)+P\left(0 \leq Z \leq \frac{80-m}{\sigma}\right) \\
& =0.2+P\left(0 \leq Z \leq \frac{80-m}{\sigma}\right)=0.3
\end{aligned}$$

에서 $\mathrm{P}\left(0 \leq \mathrm{Z} \leq \frac{80-\mathrm{m}}{\sigma}\right)=0.1$ 이므로 $\frac{80-m}{\sigma}=0.25$
즉, $m=80-0.25 \sigma \cdots \cdots$ (ㄴ)
(ㄱ), (ㄴ)에서 $3+0.52 \sigma=80-0.25 \sigma, 0.77 \sigma=77, \sigma=100$
따라서 $m=3+0.52 \times 100=55$ 이므로
$$m+\sigma=55+100=155$$
63) [정답] (4)
[해설]
확률변수 $X$ 가 정규분포 $\mathrm{N}\left(m,\left(\frac{m}{3}\right)^{2}\right)$ 을 따르므로
$$Z=\frac{X-m}{\frac{m}{3}}$$

으로 놓으면 확률변수 $Z$ 는 표준정규분포 $\mathrm{N}(0,1)$ 을 따른다.
$$\mathrm{P}\left(X \leq \frac{9}{2}\right)=\mathrm{P}\left(Z \leq \frac{\frac{9}{2}-m}{\frac{m}{3}}\right)$$
$$=\mathrm{P}\left(Z \leq \frac{3(9-2 m)}{2 m}\right)$$

이고
$$\begin{array}{l}
\mathrm{P}(Z \leq 3)=0.5+\mathrm{P}(0 \leq Z \leq 3) \\
=0.5+0.4987=0.9987
\end{array}$$

이므로
$$\begin{array}{l}
\frac{3(9-2 m)}{2 m}=3 \\
9-2 m=2 m
\end{array}$$

따라서 $m=\frac{9}{4}$
64) [정답] (3)
[해설]
확률변수 $X$ 는 정규분포 $\mathrm{N}\left(m, 15^{2}\right)$ 을 따르므로
$Z=\frac{X-m}{15}$ 이라 하면 확률변수 $Z$ 는 표준정규분포 $\mathrm{N}(0,1)$ 을 따른다.
$\mathrm{P}(X \leq 2 m-30)=0.8413$ 에서
$$\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{P}(X \leq 2 m-30) & =\mathrm{P}\left(Z \leq \frac{(2 m-30)-m}{15}\right) \\
& =\mathrm{P}\left(Z \leq \frac{m-30}{15}\right)=0.8413
\end{aligned}$$
$0.8413>0.5$ 이므로 $\frac{m-30}{15}>0$
$$\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{P}\left(Z \leq \frac{m-30}{15}\right) & =\mathrm{P}(Z \leq 0)+\mathrm{P}\left(0 \leq Z \leq \frac{m-30}{15}\right) \\
& =0.5+\mathrm{P}\left(0 \leq Z \leq \frac{m-30}{15}\right)
\end{aligned}$$
$$\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{P}\left(0 \leq Z \leq \frac{m-30}{15}\right) & =\mathrm{P}\left(Z \leq \frac{m-30}{15}\right)-0.5 \\
& =0.8413-0.5 \\
& =0.3413
\end{aligned}$$

이 때 $\mathrm{P}(0 \leq Z \leq 1)=0.3413$ 이므로
$$\frac{m-30}{15}=1, \quad m=45$$
