03 직각삼각형 ABC에서 피타고라스 정리에 의하여
$\overline{BC}^2=\overline{AB}^2+\overline{AC}^2=(\sqrt{2})^2+(\sqrt{18})^2=(\sqrt{2})^2+(\sqrt{18})^2$
$=\sqrt{2+18}=4\sqrt{2}$
$\overline{BC}>0$이므로
$\overline{BC}=(4\sqrt{2})^{\frac{1}{2}} = (2^{\frac{5}{2}})^{\frac{1}{2}} = 2^{\frac{5}{4}} = \sqrt[4]{2^5}$
직각삼각형 ABC의 넓이를 $S$라 하면
$S=\frac{1}{2} \times \overline{AB} \times \overline{AC} = \frac{1}{2} \times \overline{BC} \times \overline{AH}$
이므로
$\overline{AH} = \frac{\overline{AB} \times \overline{AC}}{\overline{BC}} = \frac{\sqrt{2} \times \sqrt{18}}{\sqrt[4]{2^5}} = \frac{\sqrt{2 \times 18}}{\sqrt[4]{2^5}} = \frac{\sqrt{9}}{\sqrt[4]{8}}$
따라서 $\overline{AH}^4 = (\frac{\sqrt{9}}{\sqrt[4]{8}})^4 = \frac{9}{8}$
